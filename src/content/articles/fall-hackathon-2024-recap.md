Our Fall Hackathon 2024 was an incredible success! Over the weekend of October 12-13, 80+ students came together for 48 hours of innovation, collaboration, and creativity. Here's a recap of this amazing event.

## Event Overview

The Fall Hackathon brought together students from all years and majors to build innovative projects addressing real-world problems. Participants formed teams, brainstormed ideas, and coded their way through two intense days of development.

### By the Numbers

-   **80+ participants** from various departments
-   **22 project submissions** across multiple categories
-   **15 mentors** from industry and academia
-   **8 sponsors** providing resources and prizes
-   **Countless cups of coffee** consumed

## Categories and Themes

Teams could choose from several categories:

**Web Applications**: Build full-stack web apps solving everyday problems  
**Mobile Development**: Create Android or iOS applications  
**AI/ML Projects**: Leverage artificial intelligence and machine learning  
**Hardware/IoT**: Integrate hardware with software solutions  
**Social Good**: Projects addressing social or environmental challenges

## Winning Projects

### First Place: EcoTrack

A mobile application that gamifies sustainable living by tracking carbon footprint and suggesting eco-friendly alternatives.

### Second Place: StudyBuddy AI

An AI-powered study companion that generates personalized quizzes, summarizes lecture content, and adapts to individual learning styles.

### Third Place: AccessiWeb

A browser extension that automatically enhances website accessibility by adjusting contrast, font sizes, and adding screen reader improvements.

## Special Awards

-   **Best UI/UX**: LocalConnect - A platform connecting residents with local businesses
-   **Most Innovative Use of Technology**: HealthPredict - ML-based early disease detection
-   **Best First-Time Hackers**: QuickRecipe - Recipe discovery based on available ingredients
-   **People's Choice**: CampusNav - AR-based campus navigation for new students
